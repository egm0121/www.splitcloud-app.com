Q

Can I export my music playlist?

Yes, you can save your playlists to a SoundCloud account.

  1. Open the Favorites section of the app for one of the players.
  2. Tap the ... more menu button.
  3. Select 'Save to SoundCloud' option
  4. The app will let you login to the SounCloud account.
  5. Confirm the open "SplitCloud" appprompt to complete the process and save the playlist.
