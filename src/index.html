The perfect app for sharing headphones and listening two different songs!

SplitCloud is a music player app that let's you play two different songs at once on your smartphone using headphones.
Stream songs and discover new music thanks to the SoundCloud® integration.
Play all your songs Splitcloud can access all the music saved on your phone.

Get SplitCloud app for free!


Top Features

  • Create multiple Playlists
  • Listen streaming songs from SoundCloud®
  • Listen 10.000+ worldwide Online Radios
  • Play all songs and albums on your device Music Library
  • Quickly switch between single and double player mode
  • Offline Support to keep listening to your music without data connection
  • Quickly add songs to favourites for each player
  • Explore Popular and Trending SplitCloud music Charts
  • Invert left and right channel for the two players in split mode
  • Independent adjustable volumes for each player
  • Discover a selection of popular playlists perfect for every mood
