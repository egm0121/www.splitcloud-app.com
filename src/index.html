Turn your iPhone into a double music player ♫

With SplitCloud Double Music Player use your headphones and listen to two different songs at once. Play and discover new music with access to SoundCloud® and your local Music Library.

Get SplitCloud for free on the App Store!


The perfect app for sharing your headphones and listen two different songs!

Top Features

  • Search any song or user profile on SoundCloud®
  • Search and listen to all songs and albums stored in your device Music Library
  • Quickly add songs to favourite playlist for each player
  • Login and save your favourite playlist to your Soundcloud® account
  • Use SplitCloud as a regular music player when your are not sharing
  • Offline Mode to keep listening to your music without data connection
  • Invert left and right channel for the two players in split mode
  • Independent adjustable volumes for each player
  • Explore Popular and Trending SoundCloud Charts
  • Discover a selection of popular playlists perfect for every mood
