Q

Why do I hear both songs mixed together?

This issue happens when your smartphone is configured to use Mono Audio
You will have to disable mono audio to get SplitCloud to work properly.
Follow these steps on Android:

  1. Open the Settings app select Accessibility from the list.
  2. Scroll down to the Audio & on-screen text section and select Mono audio
  3. Toggle the switch to 'Off'

Also if the issue persists try with a different pair of headphones.

Can I remove the ads from the app?

You can remove the ads from SplitCloud via In-App purchase on SplitCloud for Android

Removal of ads is not yet available for SplitCloud on iOS.

  2. Select 'Remove Ads!' option
  3. Once the payment is successful ads will be removed.
